PROJECTS

Flavour – UI Prototype

FlavourQ is a vibrant and intuitive mobile food ordering UI prototype designed for a smooth and satisfying user experience. This clickable prototype includes onboarding screens, login/sign-up flow, a dynamic food category interface, item details with pricing, cart management, and a final order confirmation screen. The interface uses engaging colors, clear CTAs, and minimalistic layouts tailored for users of all levels. Ideal for a food startup or mobile-first restaurant experience.

Planto – Plant Shop App

Planto is a user-friendly mobile app designed for plant lovers to explore and purchase a wide variety of plants. From indoor succulents and air-purifying greens to outdoor garden essentials, Planto offers a seamless shopping experience for plant enthusiasts. With categorized listings, plant care info, pricing, and a cart feature, users can easily browse, select, and order their favorite plants. The modern UI and vibrant visuals make the app both functional and engaging, ideal for home gardeners, urban plant parents, and eco-friendly shoppers.
